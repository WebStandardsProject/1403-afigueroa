Balboa Skate Park

San Francisco, CA, USA

About

A crowd of skateboard enthusiasts, both the young and young-at-heart, were on hand on Saturday, September 29, for the grand opening of the new skate park at Balboa Park. The skate park project, a partnership with the Trust for Public Land, was part of a larger renovation of the park that included the construction of a new playground, resurfaced tennis courts, new picnic areas and improved pathways and landscaping. Next time you're in the area, stop by Balboa Park and "Get out and skate!"

Published by: SF Rec Park, on October 2, 2012
